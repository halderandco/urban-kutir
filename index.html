About Us

Where Craftsmanship Meets Elegance

Urban Kutir is a Kolkata-based design studio dedicated to creating handcrafted luxury furniture and premium interior solutions.

We believe that a home is a reflection of the people who live in it. Every piece we create is thoughtfully designed, meticulously crafted, and finished with an uncompromising attention to detail. From modular kitchens to custom furniture, we bring warmth, sophistication, and timeless beauty to modern living spaces.

Our approach blends traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibilities, resulting in interiors that are both luxurious and deeply personal.

— Urban Kutir
